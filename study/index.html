Study Page

We are a group of Human-Computer Intercation researchers at the University of Manitoba who are interested in improving the streamer-viewer communication and to facilitate the stream management for the streamer.

We are looking for experienced Twitch streamers to help us with our research study!

We are searching for experienced streamers to use our prototype(Stream Assistant) for one of their streaming sessions and give their feedback through two interview sessions.

We have 2 study sessions prepared for the participants:

  1. Initial Interview
    • One hour stream
    • Final Interview

Total time commitment: 2-3 hours
All activities will be online

Eligibility Criteria:

  • At least 2 years of streaming experience
  • At least 100 regular viewers per stream
  • Stream chat language should be English
  • Age should be at least 18 years old

Study Procedure

The study will be online, using Zoom, Twitch.tv, and a prototype website. Your participation in this research study involves two sessions. The first session will be an initial interview; the second session includes an hour of streaming and a final interview.

Stream Assistant

We have implemented a Stream Assistant, which will help you improve your communication with the viewers and stream management. You will be using it during the one-hour streaming session, and we will be recording it and later analyzing your usage patterns and streaming performance.

Contact

If you are a streamer who meets the participation criteria and would like to participate in the study, we will be more than happy to have you as one of our participants, and you will be compensated $75 CAD in appreciation for your time.
